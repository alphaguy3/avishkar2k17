About

Humans can now not only think for themselves, but make machines ‘think’ as well. This event is all about an autonomous bot built from scratch by participants, judged on its ability to perform usual tasks. It tests level of precision in end to end designing and fabrication of the bot and intelligent use of sensors and coding.

Participation Criteria

Team size : 4

A team can have members from different colleges.

Rules

  1. You will be provided with 220V 50 HZ AC power.
  2. You can’t supply more than 120W power to your robot.
  3. Voltage must not be more than 24V between any two points.
  4. If robot is supplied with AC power, RMS voltage between any two points can’t be greater than 24V.
  5. Your Code should not be hard-coded.
  6. Organizers reserve the right to change any rules or make new as they deem fit. 10. Robot must fit into a box of dimension 25 cm x25 cm x20 cm however it can expand later during gameplay.
  7. Robots are not allowed to fly.
  8. Any kind of explosive or flame based system is not allowed.
  9. Radio jammers are not allowed.
  10. Robots must not weigh more than 5Kg.
  11. You are allowed to use readymade sensors.
